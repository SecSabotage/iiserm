The library usually opens at 9:00 AM or 10:00 AM. On regular Sundays, it's 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM, locked for lunch, then 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM. During exam weeks, hours extend until midnight (12:00 AM) and sometimes remain open all night until 6:00 AM.
